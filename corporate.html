Mission

Revive is committed to helping parents with young children reclaim their energy and vitality through safe, effective, and natural solutions. We understand the challenges of parenthood and aim to support parents by providing products that help them feel refreshed, re-energized, and rejuvenated.

Vision

In a world where parents get tired and overwhelmed because of their daily tasks easily, we at Revive believe they deserve better. All parents should have enough energy to take care of their children and spend quality time with them without compromising on their own well-being and self-care time. We strive for a world where all parents can be their best selves and reach their true potential without sacrificing their time, resources, or health, no matter how busy life gets!

Values

Ingredient Integrity
We understand that parents want to make informed decisions about the products you bring into your home and around your family. Most parents attach great importance to using products that are safe and harmless. Therefore, we are completely open and honest about all the ingredients we use in our drops, how they were produced and where they came from. We care about our customers and provide them with the information they need to feel confident about their choices. At Revive we source our ingredients in an ethical way and make sure to use sustainable manufacturing processes that don’t harm our planet.
Environmental Conservancy
At Revive, we care about planet Earth. Our planet offers many natural resources, and it is our duty to make sure we conserve them. We care about protecting our planet, not only for the current generations, but also for the future generations. Parents want and need a healthy Earth for their kids to live on. For the manufacturing of Revive products, no natural resources are depleted. From the drops to the packaging, everything is produced with awareness of the environmental impact that we’re making. Our drops are made from local and sustainable ingredients. Our packing peanuts are biodegradable, and our shipping boxes are made of 100% recycled cardboard. Besides that, we donate 10% of our total profits to Conservation International. They've helped to protect over 6 million square kilometers of land and sea. We proudly achieve all this even while adhering to the highest quality standards. Therefore, today’s parents can enjoy Revive without any limitations.
Radical Agility
Revive prioritises the ability to respond quickly and effectively to unforeseen opportunities and challenges in the wellness industry. We understand that parental needs are constantly evolving, and our team is committed to developing proactive solutions to stay ahead. We recognize that in order to stay competitive in a dynamic industry, we must be willing to take calculated risks and adapt to new situations quickly. Our team is empowered to make decisions and take action in the best interest of our customers, without being hindered by bureaucracy or red tape. At our company, we are constantly innovating and seeking out new opportunities to improve our products. Whether it's through adopting new technologies, exploring alternative business models, or collaborating with other industry professionals, we are committed to staying on the cutting edge of our field.
Parental Vitality
We believe that as a business, we have a responsibility to support parents in maintaining their physical and mental well-being. By providing products and services that promote parental vitality, we can help parents be the best version of themselves, which in turn enables them to be better caregivers for their children. When parents are re-energized, relaxed, and optimistic, they are better equipped to handle the challenges that come with parenting, and provide their children with the care and attention they need.

    Context of campaign and promotional activities

    Our brand is committed to providing consumers, especially parents of young children, with an organic, sugar-free, and healthy option for boosting their energy levels a couple drops at a time. The drops are versatile and can be added to a variety of foods and beverages, making it easy for consumers to incorporate them into their daily routines. We prioritize transparency in all aspects of our production process, from ingredient sourcing to manufacturing and packaging. By choosing our brand, consumers can feel confident that they are making a positive impact on their health, the environment, and their community.

    We have chosen to use Instagram as a social media platform for exposure for our brand "Revive" as it has one of the highest percentages of parents with young children on it and will also help us reach our marketing objectives. According to a Pew Research Centre poll performed in 2021, parents with children under the age of 18 utilise a range of social media sites to keep connected and active with their family and friends, and 41% of parents use Instagram to post images and interact with other parents (Auxier et al., 2022).

    Instagram can be a helpful marketing tool to promote our brand in several ways:

    • Visual storytelling: Instagram is a visual platform that allows businesses to tell their brand story through images and videos. This provides a unique opportunity for us to showcase our products in a creative and visually appealingly way.

    • Brand awareness and social proof: With over 1 billion monthly active users, Instagram offers a vast platform for businesses to reach a wide audience. Companies may use hashtags and promote their postings to enhance brand recognition and attract new consumers.

    • Targeted advertising: Using Instagram's advertising platform, we may tailor our ads to certain demographics, hobbies, and behaviors. This implies that we may reach out to people who are more likely to be interested in our products or services, enhancing the efficacy of our advertising campaigns.

    • Influencer marketing: Instagram is well-known for its influencer marketing, collaborating with well-known users to promote a business or product. This may be a very successful strategy to reach out to new people and raise brand recognition.

    • Engaging with customers: It allows us to interact with our consumers via comments, direct messaging, and user-generated content. We would be able to establish a devoted following and improve brand loyalty by reacting to comments and sharing user-generated material.

      • Ways this connects to our objectives:

    • Different types of posts: To reach our objectives we decided to use a combination of product posts, tips and trick posts, quote posts, recipe posts and reels to gain a broader knowledge on what people are the most interested seeing on our page and attract as many people interested in different types of posts within our target group as possible.

    • Hashtags: We used different sets of hashtags to track reach performance on different posts and attract people interested in the topics of health, fitness, healthy recipes and supplements.

    • Reels: To be able to reach as many people as possible we also took advantage or reels. For starters, because Reels are visible not only to your followers, they can help you reach a larger audience. Reels also allow you to express your creativity through the use of music, filters, and effects. Using trending audios and participating in “trends” helps the videos’s engagement and exposure.

    • Interactive stories: Interactive stories were a great way for us to increase engagement on Instagram, because they encouraged our viewers to interact with our content. Our interactive stories typically involved polls, questions, and swipe emojis that encouraged our viewers to engage with the content by answering questions or providing feedback.

      • Objectives:

    • Reach objective: Our primary goal is to reach as many parents with young children as possible. We aim to achieve this by leveraging the social media platform Instagram, to increase the visibility of our Website. Our target is to reach at least 1000 people on Instagram, collectively through all the posts, by the 9th of April, 2023.

      • Reflection: We reached 1236 people of which 1141 were non followers, so the reach objective was met.

    • Affect objectives: - We want our target audience to feel energized, hopeful, and confident about being a new parent. We aim to achieve this by highlighting the benefits of the products our brand would eventually sell, which would help them energize and get ready to tackle another day, and the valuable information people would be able to gain from our Instagram account. We would achieve that by writing informational captions (copywriting) under our Instagram posts.

    • Response objectives: Our goal is to encourage our target audience to interact with our posts on social media (like & comment) and go to our website to then further give us their email for email marketing. We aim to achieve this by creating a pop up with discounts when people give their email for email marketing

      • Learning Points

      (check appendix for marketing analytics)

    • Reels performance: We expected for the reels to reach significantly more people than the posts. Only one of the reels performed significantly better than the usual posts. What we found was that this specific real, which performed better than the rest had no text on screen at any point of the video. Not telling people the end goal of a video is crucial for the watch time. All the other reels had an explanation of the end goal in the first 3 seconds of the clip, so people already had an idea of what they were watching (Halloumi salad, Green smoothie, Smoothie bowl, etc). Lastly the text on those videos was added with an external software, and not by using the Instagram fonts available for reels, which we later found out was detrimental for the algorithm.

    • Post performance: We expected for the Recipe posts to be the ones with the highest amount of engagement, due to the informative content. That was the case consistently. However, there were a couple of product posts with nice visuals and the “tips and tricks” post which did outperform/matched the performance of the recipe posts. We also found that adding a sound/music on a static posts makes it perform a little better than usual, because the music in combination with the visuals, catches the users attention better than just visuals. On the contradictory whenever the posts were just simply “quotes” the performance was lower than the usual.

    • Stories: Just like expected, the interactive posts had the highest amounts of engagement with the public. With the most engaging one of the ones we tried being the poll story. This, we learned, is due to the fact that the only way of interaction with a normal static story is by either replying or liking it, while with an “interactive story’ you have a third way of interaction which usually really makes the viewer enticed to react.

    • Boosted post: During the duration of our campaign we boosted one of our better performing posts for a little under 10 euros, to experiment and see how much more engagement and reach a boosted post can give us, compared to the organic ones.

      • Results and what we learned:

      We got 30 profile visits from the ad alone. Taking into consideretion that the objective for the campaign was “more profile visits” and reach, and not engagement (likes and comments), we do consider the “boosted post” as a success. The reach grew by almost 10 times (from the usual 78-80 people to 762 people), even though the engagement only grew with one like during the duration of the boosted post.

      Future Planning

      If we were to continue with running this account, we would focus on incorporating actual people into our social media platform’s post, as well as showing video commercials that include real people using a product and expressing their personal experience. These advertisements would not only highlight the product, but also the eventual objective of using it.

      We want potential buyers to imagine themselves using a product and getting comparable results when they witness others enjoying its benefits, because people buy products that other people buy, for multiple different reasons such as: fear of missing out (FOMO), social proof- they may believe that if so many other people are buying it, then it must be a good product, and convenience- sometimes, people may simply not have the time or desire to research products on their own, so they trust other people’s opinions. We want to generate a sense of trust and sincerity that other types of advertising cannot reach, as well as brand loyalty.

      Also, after getting feedback on our social media from the public, a lot of people commented on the fact that they would like to see more real people talking about their experience with the product, and see more of the energetic side of the product on posts, not just the healthy benefits.

      We would also create an affiliate program for small influencers to generate an affiliate code for their followers, to create free ambassadors. For Revive, an affiliate program for small influencers can be highly advantageous. By creating an affiliate program, we can reach a wider audience of potential customers who may not have heard of our brand before. Small influencers can promote Revive's products to their followers, who are more likely to trust and engage with them, leading to increased brand awareness and potential sales. Furthermore, an affiliate program can help us establish relationships with influencers, which can lead to further collaborations and partnerships in the future. It can also be a cost-effective way for us to advertise and market our products, as we only pay commissions on successful sales, making it a low-risk investment with potentially high returns.

      Management

      Lean Canvas

      Problem

      • Parents with young children often struggle to balance the responsibilities that come with being a parent and their personal self-care needs, because of a lack of time and energy.
      • This can negatively affect their mental and physical health, often resulting in fatigue, stress, and even burnout.

      Solution

      • Revive offers products that solve sleeping problems and stress: Revive Sleep and Revive Relax.
      • These products only have natural ingredients, aren’t addicting like drugs and 100% safe to use. Using Revive is easy and quick, making it the perfect product for stressed parents.

      Customer segments

      • Single parents: single parents love to use Revive, because besides being a parent and taking care of themselves, they have the extra challenge of not having a partner to fall back on when needed. When they need a little extra energy or want to relax and take some time for themselves, they can use Revive products.
      • Parents with multiple children: having multiple children usually comes with a busy schedule. Using Revive products can help these parents to take some time to relax, or to get some extra much needed energy to get through the day. .

      Unique value proposition

      Revive offers parents with young children a variety of unique self-care products and services, including:

      • Drops that help them sleep, relax and re-energize. A collection of healthy, easy, and quick recipes they can use Revive drops for..
      • Free access to a community of like-minded parents on social media. Consumers can share their thoughts and ideas on Revive online and see how other parents use it to make the most out of their days.

      Unfair advantage

      • All Revive products are sugar- and caffeine-free, in contrast to most coffee, sports drinks, and energy drinks.
      • Despite using stricly healthy ingredients and sustainable manufacturing processes, Revive stays cheap. This is because everything in the production process happens in the Netherlands. All ingredients are from the country Revive was found, resulting in low transport costs.

      Channels

      • Influencer partnerships: Popular influencers who have children of their own, will receive Revive products to test and review. Other parents who follow them and are inspired by what they do, will get influenced by positive reviews and will want to get Revive products, too.
      • Email marketing: Customers who want to be the first to know about for example special offers and new products, can sign up for an email newsletter. Every once in a while, they will receive special discount codes, for example on their birthdays. The codes will make them want to place an order on our website.

      Key metrics

      • Customer retention rate: we care about keeping our customers satisfied, which results in a high percentage of them coming back over and over again to buy our products. Our retention rate is currently at 40%, but we always strive for a higher rate. We are consistent with posting on social media and sending out email newsletters, so customers keep on getting reminded about Revive products. Through these reminders we motivate customers to buy our products again
      • Monthly visitors to our website: we strive to have as many visitors to our website as possible. We strive to have at least 15.000 visitors per month. Besides the number of visitors, we also consider the percentage of visitors that actually buy a Revive product. To keep this percentage as high as possible, we regularly offer discounts, and make sure that our products are high-quality, so customers will buy them over and over again.

      Revenue streams

      • Monthly visitors to our website: we strive to have as many visitors to our website as possible. We strive to have at least 15.000 visitors per month. Besides the number of visitors, we also consider the percentage of visitors that actually buy a Revive product. To keep this percentage as high as possible, we regularly offer discounts, and make sure that our products are high-quality, so customers will buy them over and over again.
      • Sales revenue from recipe books. Revive will develop several books, for example a smoothie book, a vegan cookbook, a vegetarian cookbook and so forth. For every recipe in the books, the Revive drops are an important ingredient. The books will inspire consumers, and therefore sales will increase.

      Cost structure

      • Product manufacturing and sourcing costs: we strive to use sustainable manufacturing processes to create Revive products. These aren’t always the cheapest, but it is important to us to stick to our values. Besides the production process, we also only use natural and local ingredients. We get our ingredients from farmers who care about the planet and produce sustainably. The production of one bottle of Revive costs €15, and we sell bottles for €30 each. This way we make a profit of €15 per bottle. The production costs are variable costs and vary proportionally with the volume of produced bottles. If we sell 10.000 bottles in a year, that means we have a profit of €150.000.
      • Website maintenance fees: to keep our website up to date, a web developer has to be hired. It is important to update the website and add new and exciting content regularly, to keep website visitors interested in buying our products. The website maintenance fee is a fixed cost. We pay our web developer €2.700 per month.
