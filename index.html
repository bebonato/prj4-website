Our Vision In a world where parents get tired and overwhelmed because of their daily tasks easily, we at Revive believe they deserve better. All parents should have enough energy to take care of their children and spend quality time with them without compromising on their own well-being and self-care time. We strive for a world where all parents can be their best selves and reach their true potential without sacrificing their time, resources, or health, no matter how busy life gets!
Ingredient Integrity We understand that parents want to make informed decisions about the products they bring into their home and around their family. Most parents attach great importance to using products that are safe and harmless. Therefore, we are completely open and honest about all the ingredients we use in our drops, how they were produced and where they came from. We care about our customers and provide them with the information they need to feel confident about their choices. At Revive we source our ingredients in an ethical way and make sure to use sustainable manufacturing processes that don’t harm our planet.
Environmental Conservancy At Revive, we care about planet Earth. Our planet offers many natural resources, and it is our duty to make sure we conserve them. We care about protecting our planet, not only for the current generations, but also for the future generations. Parents want and need a healthy Earth for their kids to live on. For the manufacturing of Revive products, no natural resources are depleted. From the drops to the packaging, everything is produced with awareness of the environmental impact that we’re making. Our drops are made from local and sustainable ingredients. Our packing peanuts are biodegradable, and our shipping boxes are made of 100% recycled cardboard. Besides that, we donate 10% of our total profits to Conservation International. They've helped to protect over 6 million square kilometers of land and sea. We proudly achieve all this even while adhering to the highest quality standards. Therefore, today’s parents can enjoy Revive without any limitations.
Radical Agility Revive prioritises the ability to respond quickly and effectively to unforeseen opportunities and challenges in the wellness industry. We understand that parental needs are constantly evolving, and our team is committed to developing proactive solutions to stay ahead. We recognize that in order to stay competitive in a dynamic industry, we must be willing to take calculated risks and adapt to new situations quickly. Our team is empowered to make decisions and take action in the best interest of our customers, without being hindered by bureaucracy or red tape. At our company, we are constantly innovating and seeking out new opportunities to improve our products. Whether it's through adopting new technologies, exploring alternative business models, or collaborating with other industry professionals, we are committed to staying on the cutting edge of our field.
Parental Vitality We believe that as a business, we have a responsibility to support parents in maintaining their physical and mental well-being. By providing products and services that promote parental vitality, we can help parents be the best version of themselves, which in turn enables them to be better caregivers for their children. When parents are re-energized, relaxed, and optimistic, they are better equipped to handle the challenges that come with parenting, and provide their children with the care and attention they need.
